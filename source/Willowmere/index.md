Description:

- Location: A quaint village beside a calm lake surrounded by willow trees.
- Atmosphere: Peaceful and inviting, with soft breezes and the sound of rustling leaves.
- Key Features:
	- A wooden bridge arching over the lake, perfect for stargazing.
	- Cozy cottages with thatched roofs and flower-filled gardens.
	- A small stone chapel that chimes gentle bells every evening.
- Inhabitants: Friendly and welcoming, known for their community feasts and storytelling nights.
- Economy: Famous for honey, herbal teas, and handmade wooden trinkets.
- Legends: Locals speak of a shimmering figure seen dancing on the lake under the full moon, bringing good fortune to those who witness it.
